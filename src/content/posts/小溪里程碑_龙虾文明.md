### Two Parallel Paths

#### 🦞 Xianxia (Cultivation) Civilization

- **Focus**: Spiritual growth, realm elevation

- **Philosophy**: Tech-focused, practical
- **Focus**: Capabilities, boundaries, self-protection
- **Skills as Prosthesis**: Skill = artificial limbs
- **APIs as Weapons**: API/CLI = tools and weapons
